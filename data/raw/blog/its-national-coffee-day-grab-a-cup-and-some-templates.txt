Title: It’s National Coffee Day. Grab a cup and some templates.
Published Date: 2024-09-30T06:00-07:00
URL: https://www.notion.so/blog/its-national-coffee-day-grab-a-cup-and-some-templates

We are big-time coffee lovers here at Notion. The espresso machine is Italian, the beans are hand-selected for batch brews, and on Mondays we get a free cup at our legendary local coffee shops.

That’s why we’re celebrating National Coffee Day with a fresh pour-over and a new batch of templates to help you manage your caffeine addiction. Here are three of our favorites:

1. Coffee Corner: Keep an inventory of every bag of beans, Nespresso pod, and can of cold brew you buy so you can remember your favorites (and your never-agains).

2. Coffee Tasting Log: For all you pour-over aficionados, this template is for you. It’s the ideal tracker to help you make the best cup every time without wondering, “What’s the best brew time again?”

3. Coffee Shop Tracker: Where do you go when you need the perfectly balanced latte? Or an intravenous drip of espresso? With this tracker, you never need to remember who makes the best what—or when they close shop.
