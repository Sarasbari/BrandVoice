Title: Builders: how successful founders & entrepreneurs build with Notion, a startup tool guide
Published Date: 2025-03-11T00:00-08:00

We believe anyone can be a builder. But it’s not the easiest path. The builder is a little obsessed. They have an idea that can’t remain in a notebook—it’s too good. If you’ve had that feeling, you know what we’re talking about.

Welcome to the Builders Series, where we salute people who are making wonderful things and bringing them to the world. From art-marketplace innovators to dog lovers to founders—we’ll share stories of people who spot an opportunity and have the grit to seize it. Are there sleepless nights? Yes. But they’ve managed to push through and are here to tell us how.

Every Thursday, you’ll meet builders like Kendall Warson, who’s disrupting the traditional artist-gallery relationship with Cohart, and Darin Bellisario, who is using AI and a deep knowledge base to help dog lovers answer every pesky question that comes up about their mutt.

What unites these builders? Big dreams and a Notion workspace.
