Building a connected company with Bumble President Tariq Shaukat

Tariq Shaukat joined Bumble Inc. as the company’s first ever President in 2020, overseeing global company strategy and operations. He was most recently President of Google Cloud, and prior to that served in a number of roles at Caesars Entertainment, McKinsey & Company, and Trilogy Software. He’ll describe how he’s helped these enterprises lead the charge for a new generation that has higher expectations for company culture, transparent leadership, and high quality tools.

How two famous YouTube creators built their businesses on Notion

Thomas and Marina are the leading authorities in the productivity, tech, and education spaces on YouTube. Collectively, they create viral content weekly for over 9 million subscribers. Each of them will show how they manage their growing businesses’s internal operations entirely on Notion — and how you can do the same for your budding freelance practice or hypergrowth organization.
